Privacy Policy for Knowledge Grapht

Effective Date: 11/20/2025

Knowledge Grapht provides tools for instructors and students to create, view, and interact with course knowledge graphs and supporting educational content. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

We collect your name, email address, and affiliation when you create an account or sign in through third party authentication providers.

Course and Usage Data

We collect information about the courses you access, the lessons you create or view, question responses, mastery progress, and interactions with learning materials.

Content You Provide

Instructors may upload or create lessons, questions, and other course related content. Students may submit responses, feedback, or other learning activity data.

Technical Data

We collect device information, browser type, IP address, operating system, and usage logs to support performance, security, and analytics.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide core platform functionality
  • Personalize learning content and recommendations
  • Support instructor dashboards and analytics
  • Improve platform reliability, security, and performance
  • Conduct internal research and feature development
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell personal information.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process information based on one or more of the following grounds:

  • Your consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Legitimate educational and research interests
  • Compliance with legal requirements

4. How Information Is Shared

We may share information with:

  • Instructors who manage courses you enroll in
  • Authorized educational partners involved in the study or deployment of Knowledge Grapht
  • Service providers such as hosting, analytics, authentication, and storage vendors
  • Regulators or authorities when required by law

We do not share your data with advertisers.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on secure servers with access controls, encryption, and industry standard security measures. We limit access to authorized personnel and monitor systems for potential security concerns.

6. Student Data and FERPA Considerations

For institutions subject to FERPA, Knowledge Grapht acts as a school official with legitimate educational interests. Student data is used only for authorized educational purposes and is not disclosed except as permitted by law or by the institution.

7. Research Use

Aggregated, de identified, or anonymized data may be used for educational research, platform evaluation, and academic publications. Identifiable information is never disclosed in research outputs.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Instructors may request removal of course content. Students may request deletion of account data subject to institutional policies.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion
  • Restrict processing
  • Receive a portable copy of your data

Requests can be submitted to [Contact Email].
